🎓 About Me

I’m a computer science undergraduate at Southwestern University (SU) with a strong interest in data, AI, and practical problem-solving. Before transferring to SU, I earned an Associate of Science in Computer Science from Temple College in 2023.

Through research in the SURF and DREU programs, I’ve trained diffusion models with UNet architectures for procedural content generation and built a SQL-to-Python pipeline to analyze player behavior in a Minecraft-based learning environment. These projects gave me hands-on experience with real-world datasets, meaningful codebases, and collaborative, often creative, technical work.

I’m currently looking for a part-time fall internship that pairs well with my upcoming coursework in Programming Languages and Remote Sensing. I’m especially interested in roles involving data analytics, geospatial data science, software development, or applied AI. I enjoy projects that are a bit messy, a bit inventive, and deeply satisfying to solve.

  • 🛠 Tools: Python, SQL, ArcGIS Pro, GitHub Desktop, VS Code, Godot (GDScript)
